Bio

Matthew O'Donnell
If I speak in human and angelic tongues, but do not have love, I am a resounding gong or a clashing cymbal. 1 Corinthians 12:31
Praise Him with sounding cymbals, praise Him with clanging cymbals. Let everything that breaths praise the LORD! Alleluia. Psalms 150

Matthew O'Donnell is a performer, composer and instrument maker based in Los Angeles. He's a native of Los Angeles and has an academic background in Physics and works as an aerospace engineer.

Matthew's musical inspirations are Arvo Part, Francisco Lopez and all the long-string players including Paul Panhuysen and Ellen Fullman.
